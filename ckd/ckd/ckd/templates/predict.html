Healthy Kidney Tips:

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Eat a Healthy Diet: Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your meals.
  • Control Blood Pressure: Monitor your blood pressure regularly and take necessary steps to keep it under control.
  • Manage Blood Sugar: If you have diabetes, maintain stable blood sugar levels through diet and medication.
  • Maintain a Healthy Weight: Aim for a healthy BMI through diet and exercise.
  • Exercise Regularly: Engage in physical activity for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.
  • Limit Alcohol Consumption: Drink alcohol in moderation, if at all.
  • Quit Smoking: Smoking can damage blood vessels and decrease kidney function.
  • Monitor Kidney Health: If you have risk factors, get regular check-ups and tests.
  • Follow Medical Advice: Consult your healthcare provider for personalized guidance.

For Positive CKD Diagnosis:

  • Consult a Nephrologist: Schedule an appointment with a kidney specialist for further evaluation and management.
  • Follow a Renal Diet: Limit sodium, potassium, and phosphorus intake as recommended by your healthcare provider.
  • Manage Fluid Intake: Monitor fluid intake to avoid fluid overload and maintain electrolyte balance.
  • Medication Adherence: Take prescribed medications regularly and follow dosage instructions carefully.
  • Regular Monitoring: Schedule regular check-ups with your healthcare provider to monitor kidney function and adjust treatment as needed.
{% elif prediction_text == "Negative" %}

For Negative CKD Diagnosis:

  • Follow a Balanced Diet: Eat a diet low in salt, saturated fats, and cholesterol.
  • Limit Protein Intake: Reduce consumption of protein-rich foods, as excessive protein can strain the kidneys.
  • Avoid Over-the-Counter Painkillers: Non-prescription pain medications like ibuprofen and naproxen can harm the kidneys if used excessively.
  • Control Blood Pressure and Blood Sugar: Manage these factors through diet, exercise, and medication as needed.
  • Stay Active: Engage in regular physical activity to maintain overall health and prevent further complications.
